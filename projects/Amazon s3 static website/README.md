<h2>Introduction</h2>

This project demonstrates the development of a static website for <b>The Freshly Ground Cafe</B> ,  a locally owned coffee shop based in Johannesburg, created for people who appreciate quality coffee, fresh food, and a warm atmosphere.
The goal is to improve customer interactions by providing an online platform for menu browsing, booking reservations, and submitting orders. Additionally, this project showcases the benefits of hosting on AWS, emphasizing reliability, cost-effectiveness, and operational efficiency.

<h2>Restaurant Overview</h2>

Freshly ground cafe is a beloved local cafe with a loyal customer base and strong community presence. The business has built a reputation for quality coffee and exceptional service.
<p><b>Current situation:</b> The cafe lacks a robust online presence and digital platform to support modern customer expectations.</p>
<p><b>Growth opportunity:</b> Ready to expand digital capabilities to reach more customers and streamline operations.</p>
